CULTURE PATTERN

A pattern system inspired by my Armenian identity and Christianity, translating heritage into a modern visual language.

COURSE

Design & Composition

CULTURE

Armenian • Subculture: Christianity

FOCUS

Motifs • Repetition • Visual rhythm

CONCEPT

This project explores cultural identity through pattern-making. I chose my Armenian background as the culture and Christianity as my subculture, then built a repeating design system that balances structure and ornament. The final pattern is meant to feel traditional in its inspiration, but clean and modern in its composition.
