HARMONY

A prototype concept for a music-based social platform where AI recommends songs based on listening behavior, then connects users through shared taste.

TYPE

UI/UX Prototype • Concept

FEATURES

AI recommendations • Social matching • Playlist generator

PAGES

Home • Discover • Sync • Vibe

OVERVIEW

Harmony is a prototype idea for a social media platform centered on music discovery. Users receive recommendations driven by AI and listening behavior, then connect with others who share similar taste. The experience includes discovery, following and engagement, and a playlist generator that can be random or genre-based.
