MASQUERADE WALTZ

A graphic design final blending AI, vector illustration, and handwritten Armenian typography inspired by composer Aram Khachaturian.

PROJECT

Graphic Design Final

REQUIREMENTS

Elements & Principles • AI • Vector • Handmade

COMPOSER

Aram Khachaturian

CONCEPT

This piece was created as my graphic design class final. The Armenian writing on the composer is his name written in my own handwriting, representing the emotion I imagine he felt while composing his most famous work, Masquerade Waltz.
